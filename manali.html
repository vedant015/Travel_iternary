Welcome To Manali

Manali is a charming town in the Kullu district of Himachal Pradesh, India. Nestled in the northern end of the Kullu Valley, it is surrounded by the breathtaking Himalayas and the Beas River. Manali serves as a gateway to the Lahaul and Spiti district, as well as the city of Leh in Ladakh.

With a rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty, Manali is a popular tourist destination. Explore ancient trade routes, indulge in adventure activities, and soak in the serene landscapes. Whether you're a nature lover or an adventure enthusiast, Manali has something for everyone.
